Thanks for visiting Slouching Hound.

Slouching Hound is the home of high-quality beautifully made collars, pet portraits and gifts for any hound lover.

About me
My love for hounds came from a small child growing up with a house full of sighthounds to now owning my own mischievous little whippet Alfie. With this love i combined my degree in art and textiles to produce high quality bespoke dog collars, beautiful pet portraits and now other hound related products.

For my portraits I work in different mediums including pastels, acrylics and artist quality crayons and produce life like pictures for you to treasure from your own photographs.

My collars come in lots of different designs, they can be made to measure, lined or unlined.

feel free to contact me for details. . . .
